It's an epic occurrence

Epicurrence is different. It’s a mixture of invitation and challenge. It’s a community built around real, in-person, intimate relationships that centers on not only our careers but our personal lives as well. We’re inviting creative people from all over the world to come learn, grow, scrape their knees, try new things, to be vulnerable, and have an epic adventures.

Included in your ticket is access to the workshops and nightly discussions. Dinner and drinks will be included each night. Please make note, lodging and lift tickets for the mountain are not included.

During the day there will be workshops to attend. Learn things like Creating a Website in Webflow, Building your Portfolio in Semplice, Freelance Advice, How to Launch your Business, and more. If you don’t want to attend the workshops – hit the mountain during the day for skiing and snowboarding with new friends.

At night, we challenge industry leaders and attendees to intimately open up to each other in ways a day of well crafted talks could never achieve–through real, raw, conversation. At Epicurrence, everyone is a featured guest.

The details

All you need to know before joining us for this epic, one of a kind event.

What’s included?

Your ticket gives access to the hands-on workshops and intimate nightly discussions. Dinner and drinks will be included each night.

What’s not included?

Please note, travel, lodging, lift tickets, and rentals for the mountain are not included. *If you have dietary restrictions please let Dann or Kris know as soon as you can.*

Directions, Location, and Accommodations

The workshops and discussions will be held at the incredible Beaver Run Resort in Breckenridge, CO. The Resort is actually on the mountain, which means you can ski/board and come directly into the epic workshops!

Getting to Breckenridge from Denver International Airport is gorgeous drive west from the plains into the heart of the Rockies. Closer to the event we will provide a carpooling sheet. Carpooling with epic strangers is an amazing way to become epic friends. Renting a car is an effective way up the mountain, as long as you feel comfortable driving in potential winter weather. The other option is the Epic Mountain Express. https://www.epicmountainexpress.com/breckenridge-shuttles Prices range from $59-$79 one way.

There are several hotels, airbnbs, and vrbos in the area surrounding Beaver Run. If you are looking for to share a lodging experience, our slack channel is a great place to find roommates. We also have a spreadsheet for anyone interested in coordinating further. We do have a partnership discount at the Beaver Run Resort in Breckenridge.

FAQ

Do we need a laptop for the workshops?

If you want to follow along and design as you learn, then definitely bring your laptop and charger!

Do I need to know how to ski/board

Absolutely not! Breckenridge is an incredible place to learn with terrain for all levels!

Are lift tickets included?

We aren’t providing lift tickets, but there will be a sign up sheet closer to the event so we can get a group discount!

What if I don’t want to ski/board?

Breckenridge is one of the best mountain towns in Colorado, with plenty of amazing restaurants, a wonderful downtown, there is plenty to explore. You can book a tubbing session where you slide down a course on a giant inner-tube or you can go snowshoeing and explore the amazing forests! This is also why we're offering some hands-on workshops for those that want to stay at the venue.

How should I pack?

Breckenridge can get pretty chilly, so a warm weatherproof jacket is essential, along with gloves, a hat/beanie, and wool socks.

If you are skiing/boarding and don’t have snow gear, you can rent all you need on the mountain at a few locations in Breckenridge. If you need this option (jacket, pants, gloves, goggles), please rent earlier rather than the day of so you aren’t stuck without the gear you need.
